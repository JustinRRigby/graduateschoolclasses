Class 2: Network structure I -- basic network metrics

Lecture topics

Overview of basic structural metrics including degree distributions, clustering coefficients, shortest paths, centrality, motifs.

Lab

  • Basic network metrics in bipartite and unipartite networks using bipartite and igraph and calculating directly.
  • Network projections

Papers for discussion

  • Stouffer DB, Sales-Pardo M, Sirer MI, Bascompte J. Evolutionary conservation of species’ roles in food webs. Science. 2012;335: 1489–1492.
