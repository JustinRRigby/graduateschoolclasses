Class 9: Networks and transmission dynamics (infectious diseases)

Suggested reading

  • Lloyd, A. L., Valeika, S., & Cintrón-Arias, A. (2006). Infection dynamics on small-world networks. Contemporary Mathematics, 410, 209-234.
  • Chapter 7 (7.6 on networks) in Keeling and Rohani. 2008. Modeling Infectious Diseases in Humans and Animals. Princeton University Press, Princeton, NJ.
  • Lloyd, A.L. and R.M. May, Robert M. 2001. How Viruses Spread Among Computers and People. Science 292 (5520): 1316-1317.

Additional readings:

  • Roy, M. and M. Pascual. 2006. On representing network heterogeneities in the incidence rate of simple epidemic models. Ecological Complexity 3: 80-90.
  • Aparicio, J.P. and M. Pascual. 2007. Building epidemiological models from R0: an implicit treatment of transmission in networks. Proc. R. Soc. London B 274(1609): 505—512.
  • May, R.M. and A.L. Lloyd. 2001. Infection dynamics on scale-free networks. Physical Review E 64.
